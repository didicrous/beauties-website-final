Our History

Beauties and the Beat is the only female a cappella group at the University of Texas at Austin. We possess a unique set of talents and ambitions, but have joined forces to empower girls and women by slaying the stage and staying rooted in our community. Even though we have only existed since the fall of 2013, we have performed all over Austin - including being featured at Deke Sharon's Vocalosity concert this past spring - and we will be back for a third shot at ICCA this year.
