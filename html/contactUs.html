Interested in hiring Beauties to perform?

Contact our event coordinator, Lydia Chen, at the email listed above to get a price estimate. Please also note that we do not charge for every event (particularly for UT student events and volunteer events) so do not feel discouraged to reach out to us! We would love to work with you!

Interested in auditioning for Beauties And The Beat?

We typically have auditions at the beginning of every semester Fall semester, and we have auditions in the Spring on an as-needed basis. Every Fall we have an All-Acapella Information Session, where you can come and get information about all of the A Capella groups on campus. You can also request to get on our email list at the information session. If you have questions about auditions, please feel free to reach out to us via email or message us on our Facebook page (links to both are at the top of the page).
